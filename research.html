RESEARCH PROJECT

Research Festival 2025

Today marked one of the most memorable academic events of the year: the Research Festival 2025 at Saint Columban College. Under the inspiring theme, "Innovation Minds, Impactful Research: Empowering Change Through Discovery," the festival was a vibrant celebration of ideas, collaboration, and the courage to share groundbreaking work.

The atmosphere was electric with creativity and passion. Witnessing students proudly presenting their projects, exchanging insights, and supporting one another reinforced the true purpose of research—discovery, growth, and making a tangible difference.

Alongside my team, Diskar-Tech, I had the privilege of presenting our research output: "D'Marsians: Web-Based Admission & Progress Tracking with Real-Time Visualization System." Standing on the stage, explaining our system's functionality, was the culmination of countless late nights and dedicated teamwork. Every revision and challenge finally clicked into place. Held at the Youth Development Center (YDC) in Pagadian City, the Research Festival 2025 was far more than a requirement—it was a deeply fulfilling experience that strengthened my confidence and deepened my commitment to learning. It proved that meaningful ideas, when shared, truly have the power to create lasting impact..
