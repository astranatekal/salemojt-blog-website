ABOUT ME

The Journey of My OJT Experience

Panagdait! I am Hadjara A. Salem, a 23-year-old Information Technology student at Saint Columban College, specializing in Project Management. Currently, I am leveraging my organizational and professional skills through an On-the-Job Training experience at the National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP) Region IX in Pagadian City. My strength lies in leading and documenting technical initiatives, having successfully managed key academic projects such as D'Marsians, FoodTrack Management System, and PawPointment Mobile Application. This practical experience has cemented my abilities in Leadership, Time Management, Communication, and Problem-Solving. My time at NCIP further emphasizes my commitment to accuracy, organization, and professional communication. I am an adaptable and driven individual, ready to utilize my technical foundation and proven leadership skills to effectively execute projects and transition into a valuable IT professional.
